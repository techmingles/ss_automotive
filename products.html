TPV Door Seal on Body

Hot-Welded Corner + Lightweight Recyclability

A highly engineered Thermoplastic Vulcanizate (TPV) sealing solution designed for structural vehicle body openings. Features a dual-bulb profile that absorbs large door-closing tolerances while requiring exceptionally low closing forces. Clean, hot-welded corner junctions ensure a seamless, aesthetic seal contour.

Engineering Highlights
  • Hot-welded corners eliminating secondary corner molding defects
  • Dual-bulb elastomeric profile optimized for high door tolerance gaps
  • 100% recyclable thermoplastic vulcanizate elastomer base compound
  • Low door-closing force requirements enhancing user cabin premium feel

Formulated using co-extrusion technology of dense EPDM polymer with integrated steel/aluminum carrier grids. Sits tightly along the vehicle door outer beltline, preventing dust, water, and ambient humidity from entering the inner door cavity while upgrading the vehicle's side profile.
