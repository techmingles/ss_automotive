High-Fidelity Rapid Prototyping

We validate geometries, physical fitments, and dynamic closing forces before committing to heavy steel molds. Our prototyping techniques deliver functional parts that closely mirror production characteristics.

Stereolithography (SLA)

Using industrial photopolymer resins to manufacture components with superior surface details. Excellent for fitment, optical review, and structural mockups of glass run channels.

Vacuum Casting

Sillicone tooling reproduction using polyurethane resins. This process delivers low-volume functional elastomer parts with properties resembling production-grade EPDM (durometer, elongation, tensile strength).

Prototyping Key Metrics

Dimensional Accuracy ± 0.1 mm
Durometer Range (Vacuum) 40A - 90A Shore
Standard Lead Time 3 - 5 Working Days
Materials Supported PU, ABS, EPDM-like

Special Purpose Machines (SPM) & Checking Gauges

We design and construct high-precision checking gauges, checking fixtures, and custom assembly machines to guarantee that volume production profiles exactly align with customer CAD specifications.

Precision Checking Gauges

Checking gauges are critical to verify outer profiles, channel thickness, lip lengths, and corner mold angles of the EPDM Glass Run Channels under static constraints. We deliver custom gauge layouts tailored to OEM quality inspectors.

  • Anodized aluminum base configurations
  • Step and Gap measurements within ±0.05 mm
  • Stainless steel slide elements for variable glass run lengths

Special Purpose Machines (SPM)

Custom assembly mechanisms such as automated clip insertion fixtures, lip folding apparatuses, and online corner-seal compression testing modules to maximize assembly speed on Tier 1 shop floors.

  • Pneumatic-driven clamping and positioning locks
  • PLC-controlled diagnostic parameters
  • Integrated failsafe sensors for component presence
