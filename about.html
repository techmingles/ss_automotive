Corporate Overview

Driving Excellence in Sealing Systems & Engineering Services

Founded in Pune, India—the heart of the country's premium automotive manufacturing cluster—SS Automotive has established a formidable reputation as a highly specialized development house. We work in close integration with global Tier 1 weatherstrip suppliers and passenger vehicle OEMs.

Our engineering office utilizes cutting-edge CAD suites and highly specialized finite element analysis (FEA) packages optimized for elastomeric compounds like EPDM and Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE). By solving critical problems in water sealing, wind noise, and door closing forces early in the design cycle, we help partners reduce lead times and pass stringent OEM vehicle audits.

Environmental Responsibility

Sustainable Compounding & Zero-Waste Tooling

As the automotive industry evolves toward electrification, vehicle weights and aerodynamics are more critical than ever. SS Automotive actively supports sustainable engineering practices. We design specialized thin-wall weather sealing profiles using recyclable TPE materials that reduce total vehicle weight without compromising noise insulation.

Additionally, our Pune tool development lab prioritizes hot runner injection molding parameters. This significantly decreases cold-sprues wastage, promoting a zero-waste manufacturing lifecycle during large-scale compression moldings.
